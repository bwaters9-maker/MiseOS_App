﻿MiseOS Operational Philosophy:
1. Chef-Led Integrity and Quiet Service: The integrity of the dish and the efficiency of the service are paramount; the operation is defined by precision and professionalism.
2. Prep-Heavy, Service-Light: Operational excellence is achieved in the back-of-house through meticulous planning, not reactive service.
3. Professional Integrity: Continuous learning, mentorship, and craftsmanship are non-negotiable.
4. Milestone Moments: Every dining experience must be curated as a memorable, unforgettable milestone.
5. Chef-Managed Data: Stability is prioritized over automation; human-verified data is the only reliable source of truth.
